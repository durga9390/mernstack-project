THE ARTIST

Allu Arjun

About

Allu Arjun is a major Indian film star, known as the "Stylish Star," born April 8, 1982, in Chennai,
to producer Allu Aravind; he's married to Sneha Reddy, with two children (Ayaan & Arha),
famous for hits like Arya, Race Gurram, Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo,
and the pan-India blockbuster Pushpa, earning him a National Award.

Key Personal Details

Full Name: Allu Arju Born: April 8, 1982, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India Family: Son of producer Allu Aravind; grandson of comedian Allu Ramalingaiah; married to Sneha Reddy. Children: Allu Ayaan (son) and Allu Arha (daughter). Education: BBA from MSR College, Hyderabad; trained in acting in Mumbai. Career Highlights

Career Highlights

Debut: Child artist in Vijetha (1985), lead debut in Gangotri (2003). Breakthrough: Arya (2004). Pan-India Fame: Pushpa: The Rise (2021), earning him a National Film Award for Best Actor. Nicknames: "Stylish Star".

Other Ventures

Involved in endorsements and business, including a nightclub. Style & Recognition Celebrated for his dancing, acting versatility, and unique fashion sense, becoming a major style icon.

Allu Arjun's career, launched with Arya (2004), spans successful films like Race Gurram, Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo,
and the blockbuster Pushpa franchise, earning him a National Film Award
and multiple Filmfare Awards for Best Actor for his versatile roles,
from action heroes to intense characters, solidifying his status as a major Indian star.
